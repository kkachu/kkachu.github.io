A New Page

Posted on 08 February 2020 by Rizka Azmira One min read

Raison d’Etre

I’ve always had a looming feeling that I’m horrible at documenting things; which is weird. You’d think that someone with a perpetual narration going in her head would have no problem pouring her thoughts down to writing.

This has manifested in an ugly way, three months into my new job.

I am now knee deep in a long, strenuous analytics project with no doc in sight. And the cherry on top is? I have been coding it mostly in console. ¯\(ツ)/¯

Safe to say that I have shamed myself into creating this blog. Clearly, I can’t post about my work, so I’ll have to do side projects for some content. If that doesn’t work out, I’ll just keep posting gifs.
